[Arya](https://www.binhe-lab.org/members/arya-sajadian/) presented his work at the Microbiology Undergraduate Research Symposium. Great job Arya! Also shout out to his wonderful, super-supportive graduate mentor [Jessica Miller](https://www.binhe-lab.org/members/jessica-miller/)!
Arya's research (see his [poster](https://docs.google.com/drawings/d/1skHq2GxflYpaV8shS4nO7WX08pLukN3YDVTXaxg66pc/preview)) aims to quantify the level of antifungal tolerance across a panel of _C. glabrata_ strains to determine how this clinically relevant trait varies within the pathogenic yeast species. Good job Arya!
