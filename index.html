Evolution of Stress Response

Our lab studies how gene regulatory networks (GRN) controlling stress response evolve between related species. Not only is stress response critical for survival, it also need to adapt as species evolve and encounter new environments that pose different challenges. We focus on a group of commensal yeast and their free-living relatives, to learn both general rules governing GRN evolution and how microbes adapt to the host.
